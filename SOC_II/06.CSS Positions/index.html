MS Dhoni

Sport: Cricket

Country: India

Achievements: World Cup Winner, IPL Winner, ICC Player of the Year

Bio: Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and captain of the Indian national cricket team. He is regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen in the history of cricket. Dhoni led India to numerous victories, including the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011, and ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.
